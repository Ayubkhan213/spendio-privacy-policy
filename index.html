Privacy Policy for Spendio

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Spendio is a fully offline expense tracking application developed by A&K Tech.

Data Collection

Spendio does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data entered by users such as expenses, budgets, and categories is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.

Permissions We Use

POST_NOTIFICATIONS
Used to send local reminders and alerts about your expenses and budgets. No notification data is sent to any server.

SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM & USE_EXACT_ALARM
Used to schedule local notifications and recurring transaction reminders at precise times. This data never leaves your device.

RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED
Used to restore scheduled notifications and recurring transactions after your device restarts. No data is collected or transmitted.

WAKE_LOCK
Used to ensure recurring transactions and notifications are processed correctly in the background. No data is collected or transmitted.

Local Notifications

Spendio may send local notifications to remind users about their expenses and recurring transactions. These notifications are generated locally on your device and no data is sent to any external server.

Third Party Services

Spendio does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Spendio does not collect any data from anyone including children under 13.

Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are in full control of your data at all times.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
